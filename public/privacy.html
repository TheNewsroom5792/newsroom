Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Headlines Report ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We collect your email address when you sign up. For Pro subscribers, we collect payment information through Stripe, which is processed securely and never stored on our servers. We also collect the beats and Substack subscriptions you choose, and your email delivery preferences.

How We Use Your Information

We use your email to send your morning briefing and product updates. We use your beats to build your personalized feed. We never sell your data to third parties. We never use your data to serve advertising.

Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely in Supabase. Payments are processed by Stripe. Morning emails are sent via Resend. All providers are GDPR-compliant.

Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by emailing hello@headlinesreport.com. You can unsubscribe from emails at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

Cookies

We use local storage (not cookies) to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

Contact

Questions? Email us at hello@headlinesreport.com.
